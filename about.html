About NORDHEM

NORDHEM is an educational e-commerce concept built to demonstrate transparent price differentiation. Instead of hiding "good / better / best" options behind upsell flows, we put all three tiers side by side, on every product.

Our three tiers

Basic - entry-level essentials

Quality materials at the lowest possible price. Perfect for first apartments, student housing, and short-term setups.

Standard - best value

The recommended choice for most homes. Better materials, longer warranty, refined finish.

Premium - designed to last

Top-tier materials, advanced features, and our longest warranty. Built for the homes you intend to keep.

FAQ

How does shipping work?

Flat €9.99 shipping across the EU. Free over €200.

Can I return an item?

Yes - 30 day returns on unused items in original packaging.

Do tiers share dimensions?

Yes. A product's size and footprint are identical across tiers - what changes is materials, hardware, and warranty.

Is assembly required?

Most products are flat-packed. Assembly instructions are included.
